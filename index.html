Company Story

Built on field relationships, steady supply, and long-term agricultural trust.

Founded in 1996 by Prem Sharma and Pramod Sharma, New Pioneer Seeds Pvt. Ltd. grew from Village Karora, Khurja with a simple working principle: seed supply should be dependable, farmer focused, and commercially clear for every buyer in the chain.

Today, the company focuses on premium wheat seeds and premium paddy seeds for pan India demand, supporting farmers, dealers, distributors, agricultural enterprises, food processing companies, and corporate buyers with responsive service and practical product guidance.
